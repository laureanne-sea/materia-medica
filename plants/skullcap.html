Skullcap

Botanical Name

Scutellaria lateriflora

Body Systems Affected

Nervous System

Common Names

English: Blue skullcap, Mad dog skullcap

Danish: Amerikansk skjolddrager

French: Scutellaire latériflore, Toque casquée

Habitat and Growing Conditions

Shady rich areas near streams and meadows in the mountains

Active Constituents

Flavonoid glycosides scutellarin and scutellarein, volatile oils, and bitters

Properties

Sedative/relaxing (central nervous system), vasodilator (cerebrum), nerve tonic, promotes digestion

In the american tradition is Skullcap one of the most versatile of the nervines and is indicated for all nervous system disorders, especially headaches, nerve tremors, stress, menstrual tension, insomnia, and nervous exhaustion
